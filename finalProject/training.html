    Learning How to Train properly

    When starting to lift weights you will want to have a split. What is a split you might ask? A training split is another word for breaking up workouts into seperate days.


    Some training splits you might be familiar with are:

    Arnold Split

    Arnold split consist of a 3 day split repeated untill saturday. Sunday being an off day.



  • Monday - Chest and Back

    • Exersize 1 - Bench Press 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Pullups 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Chest Flys 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 4 - Barbell Rows 4 sets/10 repititions.



  • Tuesday - Shoulders and Arms

    • Exersize 1 - Shoulder Press 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Tricep Pushdowns 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Biceps curls 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 4 - pushups till failure 4 sets/10 repititions.


  • Wensday - Legs

    • Exersize 1 - Squats 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Leg extension 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Hamstring curls 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 4 - Romanian deadlifts 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Bro Split

    Bro split is also a 3 day split repeated untill sunday but this time it focuses on training your muscles twice per week.

  • Monday - Chest and Triceps

    • Exersize 1 - Bench Press 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Pec Deck 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Tricep Pushdown 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 4 - Rope pushdown 4 sets/10 repititions.



  • Tuesday - Back and Biceps

    • Exersize 1 - Lat pull Down 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Cable Rows 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Bicep Curls 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 4 - Rope curls 4 sets/10 repititions.



  • Wensday - Legs and Shoulders

    • Exersize 1 - Squats 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Leg extension 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Hamstring curls 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 4 - Romanian deadlifts 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Upper Lower

    Upper Lower is a 5 day split with saturday and sunday as your rest days.

  • Monday - Chest and Back

    • Exersize 1 - Bench Press 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - PullUps 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Cable Flys 4 sets/10 repititions.



  • Tuesday - Legs and Arms

    • Exersize 1 - Squats 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Skull crushers 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Bicep Curls 4 sets/10 repititions.



  • Wensday - chest and back

    • Exersize 1 - Incline bench Press 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Rows 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Incline DB press 4 sets/10 repititions.



  • Wensday - Legs and Shoulders

    • Exersize 1 - Squats 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 2 - Shoulder press 4 sets/10 repititions.


    Exersize 3 - Leg extensions 4 sets/10 repititions.
